bWeb
-
A desktop app for downloading videos and audio from YouTube, Vimeo, Twitter/X, Instagram, and thousands of other sites. Everything runs locally — no cloud, no accounts, no tracking.
Report a Bug GitHub
How it works
Paste a URL, pick a quality, and download. Videos save as MP4, audio as MP3, OPUS, M4A, or FLAC. Multiple URLs can be fetched back-to-back — results queue up in the side panel.
Where are my downloads?
Files are saved to your Downloads folder inside a bWeb subfolder by default. Change the location in Settings.
